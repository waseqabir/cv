Drive for Development Of Environment And Technology(DDET)

DDET is a non-governmental organisation(NGO) where our mission is to make a sustainable world with technologically efficient demandable working force, which means making people eligible to take active part towards technological advancement and also aware them about their surroundings and make them conscious of the climate change.

Universiti Putra Malaysia(UPM)

UPM, a leading research university in Malaysia, ranked 202nd in the QS university ranking. As a world-renowned centre of learning and research, UPM has attracted students and staffs from across the globe making it a well- respected global entity.

Jessore Blind Cricket Club (JBCC)

JBCC initially started to train blind people for playing cricket and to conduct cricket matches for them with the objective to bring some deserved joy in their life and as cricket has become the most popular game in Bangladesh, we used it as a tool for trying to make an effort to normalise their life. Nowadays, JBCC conducts seminars, rallies, workshops for blind people to make them more informed, capable to play their role effectively in our civilisation and we also do these same things for all the people to make them more aware, more educated, sensitive and sensible towards differently abled people.
